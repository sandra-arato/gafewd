Do you guys usually order in for lunch or go out and pick something up?

That really depends on several factors. On nice sunny days, we like to have our lunch in Hyde Park. Other times we cook a quick pasta together for all of the team. And if none of the above, we just bring our own food to the office.

How long is the lunch break?

Nobody will check how much time you take for lunch. If you do decide to take a lunchbreak longer than 40-50 minutes though, please let someone know that you'll be away for a longer time.
